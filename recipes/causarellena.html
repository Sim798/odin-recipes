Causa rellena

A tasty ceviche

Ingredients

  • 1 kg potatoes (yellow and white)
  • 3 carrots
  • 1 aji amarillo or yellow peppers
  • salt
  • palillo or turmeric
  • 100 g peas
  • 2 limes or lemon
  • 300 g of chicken
  • mayonnaise

Preparation

  1. Boil potatoes for 20 minutes with skin. If using yellow potatoes, layer them on white ones. Peel them when warm.
  2. Boil carrots, peas, and chicken. Meanwhile, saut√© aji amarillo with oil and turmeric, blend it.
  3. Shred the cooked chicken. Mix carrots, peas, shredded chicken, and mayonnaise for the filling.
  4. Mash potatoes until smooth, let them cool. Add aji amarillo, lime juice, and salt.
  5. Use the mashed potatoes as a base, add the filling, and top with another layer of potatoes. Decorate with parsley, eggs, and avocado.
  6. Causa can be served in various shapes. Chill for an hour before serving.
